I found an ideal replica of the chest from Legend of Zelda on Reddit, which then led me to an image from the store's page. This was interesting, as the two chests differed in quality quite a bit. Photos of the interior were also available, but I opted to only replicate the ouside of the chest, as the inside did not meet the project specifications (ie. being full of treasure).

Wireframe images

I couldn't decide if black or white was better for wireframe, so I took screenshots with both. I went with grey as an in-between for the video.

As every good treaure chest does, my chest contains treasure. A hoard of a soon-to-be-discontinued currency, and a key to another treasure chest! I tried four other assets before settling on these, making that decision after one completely filled the scene with keyframes and (probably) caused some irreversible side-effects.

This project was very challenging. Some things I know I could have done better, and other things could have been glitches as far as I know (for instance, at one point faces were spontaneously extruding without and keyframes being set). At the end of the day, I am satisfied with how my first model turned out. Thank you for reading!
