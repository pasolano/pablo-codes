Following the Bell is an educational RPG from the perspective of someone migrating for labor who is at risk of being subjected to modern slavery.

Much of the data used in the game is provided by the Global Fund to End Modern Slavery.

I am the lead developer, with some assistance from Professor Vierthaler, who helped start this project.

The other student working on this game who is largely responsible for the artwork and dialogue is Julia Gibson, who I am very grateful to be working with.

We hope to have a demo release done by the end of this semester. One demo already went through a round of testing, and I am currently implementing the required fixes.
